About Persona

The leading financial solution for wellness entrepreneurs.

Our Mission.

Change the way banks look at wellness entrepreneurs by providing a financial system that puts the industry's most important members first - YOU!

Our Beliefs.

Welcome to Persona. We've got you covered.

The current wellness professional's status quo isn't where it should be. You know it, we know it and the old-school banks know it. The difference between us and them? We've done something about it. Enter Persona. We provide everything your retail bank provides and much more. We'll help with payment tracking and collection, tell you how your business is doing, help you pay less taxes AND save for the ones you do have to pay. We do it all so you can focus on what matters most - grow your business in a healthy, sustainable and predictable way.

Our Community.

The Persona community is a group of like-minded individuals that are united by their pursuit of wellness for themselves and their clients. This community did not have a robust financial solution to banking and collecting payments. They've also been left behind when it comes to getting rewards, cashbacks and other perks. Today, this community is taking back control of their finances.
